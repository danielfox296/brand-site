Your lobby, restaurant, spa, and retail floor each need a different soundtrack. They all get the same one.

Hospitality environments are multi-zone by nature. The audio strategy should be too.

What We Measure

Hospitality retail operates inside a guest experience that starts before the store and continues after it. The audio environment shapes perception across every touchpoint. Entuned measures the outcomes that matter.

Guest Dwell Time

How long guests spend in retail areas, lobbies, and common spaces. Longer dwell in hospitality retail directly correlates with higher ancillary revenue per guest.

Retail Conversion Rate

Whether guests browsing the hotel shop or resort boutique actually purchase. Environmental congruence between the property's brand and its retail audio affects conversion.

Experience Continuity

Whether the audio environment supports or contradicts the brand promise across zones. A jarring transition from lobby to restaurant to spa breaks the immersion your property works to create.

How Entuned Works for Hospitality

We profile your guest. The traveler your property is built for. Their expectations, their aesthetic, the emotional arc of their stay. That profile becomes the musical foundation for every zone in your property.

Each space gets its own soundtrack. The lobby composition creates arrival energy. The restaurant soundtrack supports conversation and pacing. The spa composition reinforces calm without becoming clinical. The retail zone encourages browsing and discovery. All are psychographically connected, all are distinct in execution.

Every composition is original and license-free. We measure guest behavior across zones and optimize monthly. The result is an audio environment that feels as considered as your architecture and interior design.

Implementation Overview

Week One

Guest profiling and zone mapping. We learn your guest persona, your brand positioning, and the function of each space. You share floor plans and any existing audio infrastructure details.

Weeks Two and Three

Zone-specific soundtracks generated and shared for review. Each composition is calibrated to the purpose of the space it serves. Refinement based on your team's feedback.

Week Four

Go live across all zones. Streaming via Bluetooth or AirPlay to existing speaker systems. Behavioral measurement begins through your property management and POS systems.

Ongoing

Monthly optimization cycles. Seasonal adjustments for properties with shifting guest profiles. Reporting integrated with your operations cadence.

What we need from you

POS or property management system access for measurement. Floor plans with zone designations. Any existing audio direction or brand guidelines for the property.
