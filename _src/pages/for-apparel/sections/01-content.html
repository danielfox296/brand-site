What We Measure

Entuned tracks the behavioral outcomes that determine whether your audio environment is working or costing you money.

Dwell Time by Zone

How long customers spend in each department. Longer dwell correlates directly with higher spend per visit in apparel retail.

Average Transaction Value

Whether customers are adding that second item. Audio environment affects willingness to browse, try on, and commit to purchase.

Brand Perception Alignment

Whether the in-store experience matches what your brand promises. A disconnect between visual identity and audio environment erodes trust before a customer reaches the register.

Repeat Visit Rate

Customers who feel comfortable in a space return to it. Audio is the environmental factor most likely to create discomfort without the customer being able to articulate why.

How Entuned Works for Apparel Retail

You tell us who your customer is. Not their age bracket or zip code. Their values, their aesthetic sensibility, what makes them feel like your store is their store. We translate that psychology into musical parameters and generate original compositions that belong in your space.

The result is a soundtrack built from your customer's identity, not pulled from a catalog organized by mood tags. Every track is original. There are no licensing fees. The music exists because your store needs it.

Once deployed, we measure what changes. Dwell time, transaction value, traffic patterns. The data feeds the next round of optimization. Your audio environment gets more precise over time, not less.

Implementation Overview

Week One

Psychographic profiling session. We learn your customer, your brand positioning, and your competitive context. You share your floor plan and any existing audio setup details.

Weeks Two and Three

We generate your first soundtrack and share it for review. You provide feedback. We refine. If you have multiple zones, each gets its own composition.

Week Four

Go live. Streaming connects to your existing speaker system via Bluetooth or AirPlay. POS integration begins tracking behavioral outcomes from day one.

Ongoing

Monthly optimization cycles. Fresh compositions generated from performance data. Reporting cadence tailored to your operations rhythm.

What we need from you
