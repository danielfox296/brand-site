What We Measure

Wine retail has unique behavioral dynamics. Customers browse differently, linger differently, and respond to environmental cues that barely register in other categories. Entuned tracks the outcomes that matter.

Dwell Time

How long customers stay in the store. In wine retail, every additional minute of browsing increases the probability of a higher-value purchase and cross-category discovery.

Average Bottle Price

Whether customers are trading up. Audio environment directly affects perceived quality and willingness to pay. The research on this in wine retail is unambiguous.

Cross-Category Spending

Whether a customer buying a Burgundy also picks up cheese, charcuterie, or glassware. Dwell time and environmental comfort drive discovery across adjacent categories.

Perceived Store Quality

Customers form quality judgments within seconds of entering a space. Audio is one of the strongest environmental signals shaping that judgment, and one of the least managed.

How Entuned Works for Wine Retail

We start with your customer. Not "wine drinkers" as a category, but the specific person your store exists to serve. Their taste profile, their lifestyle, the experience they expect when they walk through your door. That psychology becomes the foundation for every musical decision.

The compositions we generate are original and license-free. They are calibrated to encourage browsing behavior, reinforce quality perception, and create the kind of environment where a customer reaches for the $45 bottle instead of the $22 bottle because the space feels right.

If your store has distinct zones, each gets its own soundtrack. The tasting area does not sound like the everyday selection. The reserve section does not sound like the front of the store. Every zone is psychographically aligned to its purpose.
