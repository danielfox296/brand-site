The Process

How the pilot works

Week 1

Discovery Call + ICP Workshop

We learn about your customer, your brand, and your goals. 60–90 minutes with your team. This is where we understand the psychology we'll translate into music.

Weeks 2–3

Build & Review Your Soundtrack

We build your psychographic profile and generate your first custom soundtrack. You review and approve before anything goes live.

Week 4

Go Live

Connect an iPad to your store speakers. Press play. That's the full hardware setup. Your test location is now streaming psychographically-optimized music.

Weeks 4–12

Measure & Compare

Music streams in your test location. Your control location runs whatever you currently play. Real-time analytics track dwell time, foot traffic, and transaction patterns.

Week 13

Review Results & Decide

We review the data together. Dwell time lift, transaction value increase, traffic patterns. If the lift is there, you convert to a paid plan. If not, you walk away with real data — no financial risk.

FAQ

Common Questions

What if I only have one location?

No problem. We do before/after testing instead of A/B testing. We measure your store's performance for 2 weeks without music, then 8 weeks with music.

What speakers work?

Any Bluetooth or AirPlay compatible system. That's most commercial speakers. Proprietary system? Let us know and we'll figure it out.

Who manages the music day-to-day?

Fully automated. The app runs 24/7. Once you press play, it streams continuously. Zero daily management required.

Can staff override the music?

Yes. Pause and skip controls in the app. But it's rarely needed — the music is designed to work all day.

What if the pilot doesn't show results?

You walk away with the data. No strings attached. You pay nothing for streaming and only pay the onboarding fee.

How much data do I get?

Complete weekly analytics dashboards — dwell time, traffic, transaction data, and ROI calculations. Everything you need to make a confident decision.
