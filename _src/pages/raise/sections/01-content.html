The Thesis
The Gap

Every retail chain plays music. Not one of them knows if it's working — because the dataset to prove it commercially has never been built. The academic literature shows music drives dwell time, willingness to pay, and ticket size. The commercial proof doesn't exist yet. We're building it.

Why Now

AI music generation hit production quality in 2024 — parametric composition, no licensing costs, no catalog constraints. Retail analytics platforms opened store-level behavioral data via API. The sensor layer already exists in thousands of stores. What didn't exist was a system that could act on it through a behavioral intervention and measure the result.

The Moat

Dozens of musical parameters × real retail environments × time = a dataset that has never existed. The more stores we run, the smarter the system gets. No competitor can replicate years of cross-retail musical data without years of access to real stores.

12-Month Milestone

3–5 pilot partners. Treatment and control store pairs generating proprietary data across 12 months. At the end: statistically significant proof that specific musical variables cause measurable lift — or we know the thesis doesn't hold. Either outcome is worth the raise.
