Common Questions

What if the pilot doesn't show results?

Our pilots are designed to demonstrate ROI. If after 90 days you don't see measurable lift in dwell time, repeat visits, or transaction size, we'll work with you to optimize—or refund your pilot investment. We're confident in our model.

What hardware do I need?

Entuned works with any existing audio system: speakers, sound systems, or in-store playlists. No hardware replacement required. We integrate with your current setup or recommend minimal, cost-effective upgrades.

How long until I hear music?

Onboarding typically takes 2–4 weeks. After your research and configuration are complete, your first soundtrack goes live immediately. Optimization then happens continuously based on real customer behavior data.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly billing with no long-term lock-in. After your pilot, you can upgrade, scale down, or exit with 30 days' notice. The only non-refundable part is the one-time onboarding investment.

How do you measure ROI?

We track dwell time, repeat visits, average transaction size, and customer satisfaction. You get weekly analytics dashboards. On Professional+ plans, our team reviews quarterly to optimize and validate your returns.

Is my customer data secure?

All data stays encrypted and on-prem if needed. We never share personal customer information. Entuned only sees behavioral signals (dwell time, foot traffic patterns) to tune soundtracks—never PII.
