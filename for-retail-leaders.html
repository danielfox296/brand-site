The Problem You Already Know

You've walked into your own store and heard music that felt wrong.

Visual Design is Intentional

Every piece of visual merchandising reflects your brand. Your lighting is calculated for ambiance. Your color palette sends a message. Your store is a carefully composed environment.

Audio is an Afterthought

Your soundtrack is a Spotify playlist someone picked two years ago. Or worse—generic content from Mood Media or Soundtrack Your Brand, organized by mood-tags that have nothing to do with your customer's psychology.

"Upbeat" is not a customer profile. "Indie rock" is not a strategy.

Generic playlists are designed for no one. They exist to fill silence. Your store deserves better than that.

What Changes With Entuned

We Profile Your Ideal Customer

Not demographics. Psychology. What do they value? What's their aesthetic? What music shaped them? What makes them feel premium? We build a psychographic model that becomes your soundtrack's DNA.

We Generate Original Music

Engineered specifically for that person. Not pulled from a catalog. Every track is original composition created for your customer's psychology, values, and sensibilities.

Your Audio Becomes as Intentional as Your Visuals

The same rigor you apply to merchandising, lighting, and design now applies to your acoustic environment. Every element aligned. One cohesive experience.

Multi-Zone: Different Departments, Different Soundtracks

Unique to Entuned. While competitors force one playlist across your entire store, we enable simultaneous soundtracks in different zones. Activewear doesn't need the same vibe as accessories. Your premium section doesn't sound like clearance.
