What Changes With Entuned

We Profile Your Ideal Customer

Not demographics. Psychology. What do they value? What's their aesthetic? What music shaped them? What makes them feel premium? We build a psychographic model that becomes your soundtrack's DNA.

What Onboarding Looks Like

From kickoff to live is four weeks. Practical. Straightforward. Built for retail operations.

Week 1

ICP Workshop

60–90 minutes with your team. We dig into your ideal customer's psychology, values, lifestyle, and aesthetics. This conversation becomes the blueprint.

Weeks 2–3

Generation & Refinement

We translate your ICP into psychographic parameters, then generate your first soundtrack. We share it, you provide feedback, we refine. Two iterations minimum.

Week 4

Go Live

iPad connects to your speakers via Bluetooth or AirPlay. Press play. Your store's soundtrack is now live.

Real-time analytics dashboard shows dwell time, foot traffic patterns, and engagement spikes. We monitor what works and generate new tracks quarterly to keep your soundtrack fresh and effective.
