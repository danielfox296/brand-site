The App

The Entuned iOS app is your store's spatial zone controller. Define your zones, assign speaker groups, and stream engineered music tailored to each zone's customer psychology.

Zone Management: Set up as many zones as you need (depending on your plan). Each zone gets its own psychographic profile. Dorm section, living room, luxury seating—all streaming custom soundtracks simultaneously. No compromise. No dilution.

Real-time dashboard shows you what's happening right now: customer dwell time, foot traffic patterns, engagement spikes, and sales correlation—all tracked by zone.

One-tap controls: Play, pause, adjust volume, switch between profiles, or fine-tune each zone's vibe whenever you want.

Questions?

How long until I see results?

Customers report measurable engagement lift within the first 2–3 weeks. Dwell time increases. Foot traffic patterns become visible in real-time analytics. By week 4–6, you'll see correlations with sales data.

What if I want to change the music style?

Easy. You can adjust the psychographic profile anytime through the app. Shift toward more energy, more sophistication, different aesthetic values—whatever your store needs. The AI generation re-composes music based on the new parameters.

Do I need special hardware?

Nope. Any modern iPad or iPhone + any Bluetooth or AirPlay speaker system. If you already have WiFi in the store and a speaker, you're good to go. Total setup cost is zero (beyond the app subscription).

Is there a contract?

The Professional and Enterprise plans are month-to-month. Essentials tier is flexible. No long-term lock-in. If the music isn't working for you, you can cancel and move on. (But we're confident you'll stay.)
