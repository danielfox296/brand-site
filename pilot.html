How the Pilot Works

Week 1

Discovery Call + ICP Workshop

We learn about your customer, your brand, and your goals. 60–90 minutes with your team. This is where we understand the psychology we'll translate into music.

Weeks 2–3

Build & Review Your Soundtrack

We build your psychographic profile and generate your first custom soundtrack. You review and approve before anything goes live.

Week 4

Go Live

Connect an iPad to your store speakers. Press play. That's the full hardware setup. Your test location is now streaming psychographically-optimized music.

Weeks 4–12

Measure & Compare

Music streams in your test location. Your control location runs whatever you currently play. You measure both. Real-time analytics dashboard tracks dwell time, foot traffic, and transaction patterns.

Week 13

Review Results & Decide

We review the data together. Dwell time lift, transaction value increase, traffic patterns. If the lift is there, you convert to a paid plan. If not, you walk away with real data—no financial risk.

What We Measure

Average Dwell Time

Test location vs. control location. How much longer are customers staying when they hear music optimized for their psychology?

Average Transaction Value

Are customers buying more per visit? Willingness to pay is directly influenced by audio environment priming.

Items Per Transaction

Does the soundtrack encourage customers to browse longer and add more to their basket?

Foot Traffic Patterns

Peak hours, visit frequency, and traffic flow distribution. Does music draw more customers in and keep them longer?

Return Visit Frequency

If trackable through your POS or loyalty program. Do customers come back more often when they've experienced music designed for them?

What You Need to Get Started

iPad or Tablet with WiFi

We provide the app. Any modern iPad or tablet works. This runs continuously and can be tucked behind a counter or in your office.

Bluetooth or AirPlay Speakers

Most commercial speaker systems work. If you have any audio system already installed, we can integrate with it.

Two Comparable Store Locations

Test location + control location. Similar demographics, traffic, and product mix. If you only have one location, we can do before/after instead of A/B testing.

Access to POS & Analytics Data

Transaction data, hourly traffic counts, and (if available) customer loyalty program data. This is how we measure ROI.

60–90 Minutes for ICP Workshop

Your team meets with us to define your ideal customer profile. This is the foundation for everything—worthwhile time investment.

Common Questions

What if I only have one location?

No problem. We do before/after testing instead of A/B testing. We measure your store's performance for 2 weeks without music, then 8 weeks with music. You'll see the lift clearly.

What speakers work?

Any Bluetooth or AirPlay compatible system. That's most commercial speakers. If you have something proprietary, let us know and we'll figure it out.

Who manages the music day-to-day?

Fully automated. The app runs in the background 24/7. Once you press play, your music streams continuously. Zero daily management required.

Can staff override the music?

Yes. There are pause and skip controls in the app if staff ever need to pause or manually jump to another track. But it's rarely needed—the music is designed to work all day.

What if the pilot doesn't show results?

You walk away. We'll give you the data. No strings attached. You pay nothing for streaming and only pay the onboarding fee if you learned something valuable about your customer.

How much data do I get after 90 days?

Complete weekly analytics dashboards showing dwell time, traffic, transaction data, and ROI calculations. You'll have everything you need to make a confident decision about converting to a paid plan.

Ready to run the test?

90 days. No upfront cost for music. Real data. If it works, you know exactly what you're getting into. If it doesn't, you learned something about your customer.
