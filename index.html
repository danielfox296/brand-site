Your store plays music for no one.

Right now, your background music is a coin flip. Generic playlists from a catalog that knows nothing about your customer. But the research is clear: when music matches the shopper, they stay longer, spend more, and come back.

From customer psychology to store soundtrack.

Entuned profiles your ideal customer — their values, their aesthetic, what moves them — and creates original, brand-aligned music to influence their behavior in your store. Not a playlist, a soundtrack that speaks to who your customer actually is.

The result: longer dwell time, bigger baskets, stronger brand perception. We're proving it in real stores.

What the research says.

Your Customers Stay Longer

Music matched to your customer's psychology slows them down, increasing dwell time (and as a result, product exposure and social proof to onlookers). Published research shows dwell time increases of 15–25% when tempo and genre match the customer profile.

Their Baskets get Bigger

When the environment is aligned, customers buy more. In published studies, genre congruence alone increased willingness to pay by 8–12%. The right tempo, the right mode, the right production style. It compounds.

Your Brand Gets Dimension

No more generic playlists that could belong to anyone. Entuned builds your brand's sonic identity as distinctive as your visual brand. Zero licensing fees.
