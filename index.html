The Science

Music drives behavior.

Decades of research show music isn't decoration — it's a behavioral lever. Here's what the science tells us:

Tempo & Dwell

Slower tempos (60-80 BPM) increase shopping time; faster tempos (120+ BPM) accelerate decisions. Tune to your customer's pace.

Genre Congruence

When music aligns with a customer's identity and values, willingness to pay increases 8-12%. Mismatch triggers departure.

Luxury Priming

Classical and refined instrumental music prime luxury perception and increase average transaction value — even in non-luxury segments.
