From psychology to playlist in four steps.

01

We profile your ideal customer.

This isn't demographic boxes. We dig into the psychology of who you actually serve. What do they value? What's their aesthetic? What music did they listen to at 18? What inspires them? What makes them feel premium?

Through a detailed onboarding conversation, we build a rich psychographic model of your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP). This becomes the DNA of your store's soundtrack.

02

Our engine translates psychology into music.

Your ICP isn't a playlist—it's a set of instructions. Our proprietary translation layer converts psychological attributes into musicological parameters: tempo, key, instrumentation, harmonic complexity, lyrical themes, genre DNA.

A minimalist customer values space and clarity—we generate music that breathes. A luxury-conscious customer wants refinement—we compose orchestral elements that prime that perception. Every psychographic dimension maps to a musical choice.

03

AI generates original music for your store.

We don't pull from a catalog. Every track in your soundtrack is original composition, generated specifically for your customer's psychology. No licensing fees. No ASCAP headaches. No track repeats across stores.

Your music is as unique as your customer. And it evolves. As we learn more about what works in your space, we fine-tune the generation parameters and create music that gets better at moving your needle.

04

Stream it. Measure it. Optimize it.

Download the Entuned iOS app, connect to your speakers, press play. That's it. The app streams your psychographically-optimized soundtrack continuously, 24/7.

Real-time analytics dashboard shows you dwell time, foot traffic patterns, peak engagement hours, and sales correlation. We see what's working. You get continuous optimization through behavioral feedback loops — the music gets smarter over time.
