What Entuned Does

Entuned streams AI-generated, psychographically-targeted music into retail stores via an iOS app. We generate music in real-time that adapts to shopper psychology, store dynamics, and zone-level behavior — not static playlists adjusted by traffic volume.

Our proprietary translation engine converts behavioral and environmental data into music-generation parameters, creating a dynamic loop between retail analytics and generative audio.

The Integration Opportunity

We want to integrate RetailNext's zone-level analytics APIs — occupancy data, shopper demographics, dwell time by zone, foot traffic patterns, and conversion metrics — directly into our behavioral feedback loop.

This creates a data-driven music generation system where each zone's music adapts not just to traffic volume, but to psychographic profiles, moment-to-moment behavioral signals, and measurable retail outcomes.
