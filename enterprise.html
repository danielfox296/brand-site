Enterprise

The retail analytics industry spent $1.8 billion on sensors and measurement. Entuned is the action layer.

Every Store Teaches the Next One

RetailNext, Pathr.ai, and dozens of analytics platforms can tell you what happened in your store. Foot traffic dropped. Dwell time fell. Customers left the premium section early. None of them can do anything about it in real time. Audio is the only intervention that costs nothing to deploy and changes the environment instantly.

At fleet scale, Entuned becomes a compounding data asset. Your fifth store benefits from what the first four discovered. Your fiftieth operates on intelligence no competitor starting from zero can replicate. After 90 days, your network surfaces correlations between musical parameters and sales outcomes that a single location would take years to find.

Your stores employ thousands of people who hear your soundtrack 2,000 hours a year, 10x longer than any customer. A 3-4 hour playlist loop means your staff hears the same songs 50 times per month. Fleet-scale retail music strategy fixes the employee experience alongside the customer one.

Fleet Learning

Every store is a sensor platform. Regional patterns, seasonal dynamics, and format-specific insights compound across your fleet. Your VP of Store Operations gets cross-location performance comparison and aggregate behavioral data that improves with every new location.

Multi-Zone at Scale

A single store often serves multiple customer types. West Elm has a $40 dorm pillow section and a $3,000 sofa section. Those are different customers who need different music. Only original, created-to-order music makes simultaneous multi-zone soundtracks possible. At enterprise scale, zone strategies tested in one region roll out to hundreds of stores overnight.

RetailNext Integration

Entuned plugs directly into RetailNext's zone-level foot traffic, dwell time, and conversion data via API. Your existing analytics infrastructure becomes the measurement layer. We become the action layer. Same pattern RetailNext validated when they acquired Pikato. Same pattern Standard AI followed when they acquired Pathr.ai. Analytics platforms always acquire the response surface.
