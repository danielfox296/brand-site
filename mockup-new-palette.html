Proven Results

What the research says.

Your Customers Stay Longer

Music matched to your customer's psychology slows them down, increasing dwell time and product exposure. Researchers have documented dwell time increases of 15-25% when tempo and genre match the customer profile. Every additional minute in the store is another chance to convert.

Their Baskets get Bigger

Areni and Kim (1993) and North, Hargreaves, and McKendrick (1999) showed that identity-congruent music increases willingness to pay by 8-12%. At an $85 average transaction across 4,000 monthly visits, an 8% lift adds $27,200 per month. The right tempo, the right mode, the right production style. It compounds.

Your Brand Stops Borrowing Someone Else's Voice

Licensed playlists carry someone else's brand, someone else's lyrics, someone else's associations. You hired a lighting designer. You spec'd the paint color. The music should have the same standard. Entuned builds your brand's sonic identity from scratch. Zero licensing fees.
