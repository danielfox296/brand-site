The Data-Driven DJ for Retail

entuned.co

The Problem

Every retail store plays music. Almost none of it is optimized.

Chains pay $15–$50/mo for generic streaming. Music is chosen by intuition, not data.

No visibility into whether music helps or hurts sales.

The real cost isn't the streaming bill — it's the revenue lost every day to music that doesn't speak to the customer in front of it.

Why Now

Two things changed in 2024 that made this company possible.

Generative AI Music

AI music generation hit production quality in 2024. For the first time, music can be composed at the variable level — specifying tempo, harmonic density, rhythmic complexity, instrumentation, and dozens of other parameters — without licensing costs, human composers, or catalog constraints. The entire library is generated to spec.

Retail Analytics APIs

Retail analytics platforms now expose store-level behavioral data via API — foot traffic, dwell time, heat maps, zone transitions. The sensor layer already exists in thousands of stores. What didn't exist was a system that could act on that data through a behavioral intervention and measure the result. That's the gap.

The Real Gap

The entire category competes on music delivery and scheduling. Dayparting — playing different music at lunch than at 9am — is the industry's most sophisticated feature. Not one player measures whether any of it actually works.

No existing service integrates with retail behavioral data. None optimize for conversion or dwell time. The category ceiling is where Entuned's floor begins.

White Space

Streaming giants and retail analytics platforms have all ignored this intersection.

The streaming giants have the music. Retail analytics platforms have the data. Nobody has connected them. That gap is the company.

Business Model

Free pilots. Paid performance. Compounding data.

Pilot (Year 1)

Free to pilot partners. Entuned replaces existing streaming spend at zero cost. The value exchange: we get access to behavioral data. The retailer gets optimized music and a measurement layer they've never had.

Post-Proof (Year 1–2)

$300–500 per store per month. Replaces $15–50 streaming at a premium — but the product isn't music, it's a measurable performance channel. At $100K monthly store revenue, even a 1% lift is $1,000/month. We capture 30–50% of the value we create.

Performance Tier (Year 2+)

Base SaaS plus a performance bonus above a proven lift threshold. Uncapped upside for both parties. The more stores in the network, the smarter Miles gets, the higher the lift, the more we earn.

Downstream revenue: data licensing · generative model API · brand insights product
